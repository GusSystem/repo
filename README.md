# Repo
Hello there ! Thanks for coming here. On this repository, you'll find some packages such as : 
 - Tweaks to make old iOS more usable
 - Tweaks to customize your device even more
 - Tweaks to hack old games
 - Even more in the future !

This repository is made for my old iDevices, such as my iPhone 5s (iOS 12 and 10), my iPad 2 (iOS 8.4.1 and 6.1.3), and my iPhone 4s (iOS 6.1.3 and 8.4.1).

# How do I add it ?
Addming a repo in Cydia is ez. Just go to sources, click add, and add https://gussystem.github.io/repo. Simple, huh ?
